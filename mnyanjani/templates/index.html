what you can know from our school!

Mnyanjani Secondary School is a co-educational institution offering both science and arts subjects. We are dedicated to providing quality education in a conducive learning environment, supported by state-of-the-art facilities.

  • Modern science laboratory for practical learning.
  • Wide range of academic subjects including sciences and arts.
  • Experienced and dedicated teaching staff.

Mnyanjani Secondary School is located 12.1 kilometers from Tanga city Bus Terminal at Kange, in the Tanga Region. Our school has an enrollment of 750 students, 25 teachers, and 3 non-teaching staff, all working together to foster a community of academic excellence and holistic development.

Why Choose Mnyanjani Secondary School?

At Mnyanjani Secondary School, we provide a holistic education that fosters academic excellence and personal growth. Our commitment to quality education and a supportive environment makes us the ideal choice for students seeking to excel.

01 What programs do we offer?

We offer a diverse range of programs, including science and arts subjects, catering to the varied interests and talents of our students. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for national examinations and future academic pursuits.

02 How does Mnyanjani Secondary School support students?

Our dedicated teaching staff provides personalized support to each student, ensuring they receive the guidance needed to succeed academically and socially. We also offer various extracurricular activities to promote overall development.

03 What facilities are available at the school?

Mnyanjani Secondary School boasts modern facilities, including a well-equipped science laboratory, a spacious library, and sports facilities, providing students with an enriching environment for learning and growth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Mnyanjani Secondary School, including admissions, academic programs, school facilities, and more.

How can I apply for admission at Mnyanjani Secondary School?

To apply for admission, please visit our Admissions Office in our school or download the joining instructions form from our website. Completed forms should be submitted with all necessary documents by the specified deadline.

What subjects are offered at the school?

Mnyanjani Secondary School offers a variety of subjects in both Science and Arts streams, including Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, History, Geography, English, and Kiswahili.

Does the school provide boarding facilities?

Yes, we provide safe and secure boarding facilities for both boys and girls, with accommodations monitored by our staff to ensure a comfortable and supportive environment.

What extracurricular activities are available?

Students can participate in various activities, including sports like football and basketball, academic clubs, and cultural events. These activities help foster teamwork and personal development.

How can parents contact the school?

Parents are encouraged to contact the school administration directly via our main phone line or email. We also hold regular parent-teacher meetings to ensure open communication.

How to Join Mnyanjani Secondary School

Below are the guidelines and documents required for new students joining Mnyanjani Secondary School. Please review the instructions carefully and download the necessary documents.

  • 1. Review the admission requirements for your class.
  • 2. Complete the admission forms provided.
  • 3. Gather all required documents (birth certificate, previous school reports, etc.).
  • 4. Submit all forms and documents to the school office by the due date.

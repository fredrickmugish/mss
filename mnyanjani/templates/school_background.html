School Background

Established in 2007, Mnyanjani Secondary School is a ward school located in Mnyanjani Ward, Tanga, Tanzania. Built through a collaboration between the government and the local community, the school serves both boys and girls from Form 1 to Form 4, providing education in both arts and science streams.

Our Mission

Mnyanjani Secondary School strives to empower students through quality education, instilling values of integrity, discipline, and academic excellence. We aim to provide a supportive environment where each student can excel in their chosen field of study.

Our Vision

To become a leading secondary school that fosters holistic development, ensuring our students are well-prepared to contribute positively to society.
